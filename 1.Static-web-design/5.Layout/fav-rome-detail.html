Rome – synonymous with romance, it’s all in the name. As one local explained to me – if you reverse the word ‘Roma’ (the Italian word for Rome) you get ‘Amor’ – meaning love. And love is something that resonates throughout the entire city. Especially evident at nighttime, the romantic undertones permeate the place as tangibly as pizza or pasta.

As intertwined as food and love may be, Rome is a place for individual passions, and whether it is architecture, dining, history or character you crave – it boasts it all in bountiful stores.
